Our CCTV & Security

Protect your property with a professionally designed and installed CCTV system from JKJ Solartech and Security. We assess your property, identify vulnerable areas and install a system that provides full coverage — day and night.

From basic home setups to multi-camera commercial systems with remote viewing, we install, configure and maintain complete security camera solutions that give you real peace of mind.

Services Include:

  • HD analogue CCTV camera installations
  • IP and PoE camera network installations
  • DVR and NVR recording system setup
  • Remote viewing setup on smartphones and computers
  • Night vision and infrared camera installations
  • Pan-tilt-zoom (PTZ) camera systems
  • Outdoor weatherproof camera mounting
  • Multi-site and commercial surveillance systems
  • CCTV system upgrades and expansions
  • Cable management and concealment
  • System servicing, troubleshooting and repairs

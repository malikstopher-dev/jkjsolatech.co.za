About JKJ Solartech and Security

About JKJ Solartech and Security

JKJ Solartech and Security (Pty) Ltd is a registered Johannesburg-based company providing solar, electrical and security solutions for residential and commercial clients.

We help property owners improve safety, reduce downtime and prepare for load-shedding through reliable backup power systems, professional electrical work and practical security installations.

Our work is guided by clean execution, honest communication and long-term reliability. Whether it is a small repair, a solar upgrade, CCTV installation or a complete security setup, we approach every project with care and professionalism.

We power properties. We protect people. We build confidence.

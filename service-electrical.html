What We Offer

Our Electrical Services

JKJ Solartech and Security provides comprehensive electrical services to residential and commercial clients across Johannesburg. Our certified electricians are equipped to handle everything from simple repairs to full electrical installations — safely, efficiently and to SANS standards.

Whether you need an urgent fault fixed, a new DB board installed, wiring for a renovation or a Certificate of Compliance for property transfer, our team delivers reliable, professional results every time.

Services Include:

  • Residential and commercial wiring & rewiring
  • Distribution board (DB) upgrades and installations
  • Electrical fault finding and diagnostics
  • Socket, switch and light fitting installations
  • Certificate of Compliance (COC) for property sales
  • Earth leakage and circuit breaker replacements
  • Outdoor and security lighting installation
  • 3-phase and single-phase electrical work
  • Emergency electrical repairs — 24/7 availability
  • Pre-purchase electrical inspections
  • Energy efficiency audits and upgrades
